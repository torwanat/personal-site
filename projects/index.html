My projects

Here are some of the projects I've worked on. I enjoy tinkering with different technologies and frameworks, so the projects are quite varied.

bl00m

Artificial flower that opens when it detects light. Powered by Raspberry Pi Pico, it is a clean and complete design, with natural look. The assembly is also easily accessible, and offers a high degree of modularity. Read more about the materials required and the development process here, or check out the code on github.

Chinese Game

Chinese game written in Angular with PHP backend. Play the classic tabletop game with 2 - 4 players. Includes automatic matchmaking, multi langauge support and more. Read more about it here, or play it online at chinese.torvan.eu.

Tic Tac Toe app

Mobile app for playing Tic Tac Toe, both locally and online. Available game modes: local PvP, local PvE and online multiplayer. Multiplayer works with automatic matchmaking built with Firebase. An older app, but still quite cool. Read more about it here.
